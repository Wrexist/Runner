# Privacy Policy — Critter Dash

_Last updated: 2026-06-05_

Critter Dash is designed for the **Apple Kids Category** and built to be safe
for children by default. This policy is intentionally short because the app
intentionally does very little with data.

## Data we collect

**None.** Critter Dash does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information. There are:

- **No** user accounts or logins.
- **No** analytics or tracking SDKs.
- **No** advertising or ad identifiers.
- **No** network requests that send your data anywhere.
- **No** social features, chat, or user-to-user contact.

## Data stored on your device

The game saves a small file **locally on the device only**: high score,
unlocked critters, sound and difficulty settings, a first-run tutorial flag, and
lifetime play stats (total critters rescued and number of runs played). None of
this is personal information, it never leaves the device, and it is removed when
you delete the app.

## Purchases

The app offers a single optional one-time purchase ("Unlock All Critters")
handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We never see or store payment details.
A parental gate (an arithmetic question only an older child or adult can pass)
appears before any purchase screen.

## App Store privacy label

This app declares **"Data Not Collected"** in the App Store privacy nutrition
label, consistent with the above.

## Compliance

This app is built to comply with COPPA (US) and the UK/EU age-appropriate
design codes: no behavioral profiling, no data collection from minors, no
third-party tracking.
