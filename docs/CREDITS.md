# Credits & Asset Licenses

Log every third-party asset here as you add it — even CC0 ones. This is your
single source of truth for App Store review, the in-app Credits screen, and your
own peace of mind. Fill in **Source URL**, **Author**, and **License** the moment
you download something; an empty row means "not sourced yet."

**License cheat-sheet**
- **CC0 / Public Domain** — no attribution required (preferred). Still logged here.
- **CC-BY 4.0** — allowed, but you **must** credit the author (a row here + the
  in-app Credits screen satisfies this). Avoid for a kids' app unless you love it.
- **Kenney / Pixabay license** — royalty-free, no attribution required; treat like
  CC0 but paste the item URL so the exact terms are traceable.
- ❌ **Never** use "free for personal use only," unclear, or AI-scraped-of-unknown-origin assets.

> Keep the **license file** Kenney/Quaternius ship inside each pack. Drop a copy
> at `themes/<id>/<models|audio>/LICENSE.txt` alongside the assets.

## Attribution shown in-app
Any asset whose license is **CC-BY** (not CC0) MUST appear on the in-app Credits
screen. CC0 assets are optional to display but appreciated. Keep the wording the
license file asks for verbatim when one is provided.
