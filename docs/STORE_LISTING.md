## Names

- **Subtitle (≤30 chars):** `Gentle rescue runner for kids`

## Promotional text (≤170 chars, updatable without review)
`A calm, colorful runner for little hands. Match a gem, reach its cage, rescue a friend! No ads, no tracking — just kind, joyful play.`

## Description
```
Critter Dash is a gentle lane-running game made for young children and the
grown-ups who care about what they play.

The friendly twist — the Rescue Run:
Grab a colored gem to carry its color, then reach the matching-color cage to
RESCUE a critter. Each rescued friend joins a happy parade behind you!

Made to be kind:
• No ads. No tracking. No data collected — ever.
• No timers, no pressure, no "just one more" tricks.
• A gentle three-stumble loss — little ones can always recover.
• Easy mode by default; bump it to Normal when they're ready.

Made for little players:
• One-finger play: tap a side or swipe to move. No reading required.
• Color-blind friendly: every color also has its own shape.
• Reduce-Motion option for sensitive eyes.
• Big, clear buttons and a happy, calming look.

Fair and simple:
• Free to play the whole game.
• One optional purchase unlocks all critters forever — always behind a
  grown-up gate. Restore Purchases included.

Collect and replay:
• Earn new critter friends by playing.
• See them all in "My Critters."

Built for the Apple Kids Category and designed around COPPA and age-appropriate
guidelines. Happy rescuing!
```

## App Review notes (paste in submission)
```
Kids Category, designed for COPPA/age-appropriate compliance.
- No data collection, no analytics, no ads, no third-party SDKs, no external links.
- Save data is local-only (user://).
- One non-consumable IAP ("Unlock All Critters") + Restore Purchases.
- A PARENTAL GATE (arithmetic question) precedes the Shop and the Reset action.
  To reach it: Start → My Critters → Unlock All Critters → answer the math gate.
- Difficulty defaults to Easy; gentle 3-stumble loss, no harsh feedback.
```
